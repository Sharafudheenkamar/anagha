info@votte.com

  • You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Select whether you are a voter or an election coordinator and follow the instructions to register.

  • If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your account email, and check your inbox for a reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password, then return to the login page to sign in. If you don’t receive the reset email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance..

  • If you registered as a voter, you’ll need to wait for approval from an election coordinator to verify your profile. Once approved, you’ll be notified, and you can start using the site. If you registered as an election coordinator, your account will need to be verified by an admin. After admin approval, you’ll receive a notification, allowing you to access coordinator-specific features. Please allow some time for approvals, and contact support if you experience any delays.

  • To cast your vote, approach a coordinator’s computer where the election coordinator will open the voter window for you. Once the coordinator approves, you’ll be able to mark your vote. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully to ensure your vote is accurately recorded. Please remember to maintain fair voting practices and follow the coordinator’s guidance throughout the process..
